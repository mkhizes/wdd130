Our next goal at Lubanzi Piggery Farming is Free Range farming

What is free range farming?
In a free-range system pigs are kept on big, open land or in large camps where they can roam freely on pasture or grasslands. Pigs are moved to new and fresh camps at specific intervals. Shelter must be provided to protect pigs from the sun, wind and rain. Free range pig farming is one of the best ways to ensure that your animals get all of the nutrition they need. All of that rooting, digging and foraging in the dirt provides pigs with many of the elements they need. Even those who raise pigs indoors in confinement settings recognize this to some degree. pigs are kept outdoors in big enough camps where they can eat, sleep and play outside for their entire lives to allow them to express their natural instinctive behaviours.” What are the advantages of free-range pig farming? It is not always easy or straight forward to care for the pigs in this way, but the benefits to welfare are vast. Being outdoor enables the pigs to perform instinctive behaviors, reducing stress to pigs, increasing welfare, and inturn, improving quality of the meat.
