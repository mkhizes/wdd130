HISTORY ABOUT LUBANZI PIGGERY FARMING

Lubanzi Piggery established in 2019 in the area of Danganya,
Durban South-Coast, in the province of KwaZulu-Natal in South Africa.
The piggery started with a gilt which produced only six piglets and from
those six piglets the barn got expanded and as the pigs number increases we
then bought another gilt which produced 12 litter.
The company had been supported by the department of Agriculture of KwaZulu-Natal
in the Umbumbulu district, by providing a veterinary doctor and a feed for the pigs.
In the mid year 2019 we started to sell four weeks old piglets going to twelve weeks
old piglets with the aim of helping those young and upcoming farmers who have more interest
in piggery.

This opened an opportunity for Lubanzi to train and mentor these farmers who are freshers
in the industry, we also began selling a piggery starter which included the following: two
Piglets, dewormer and iron medicines, 50 kg starter feed, notebook(records book), a texttile
tape-measure. We managed to reach out to a number of farmers while at the same time building
ties with other experienced farmers learning new ideas on how to raise pigs with affordable
cost, this made Lubanzi Piggery Farming take a step towards marketing the company through
social media on facebook, that is when we then became more busier than any other time more
especially on selling whole pig carcases to the street vendors and other business people
who runed food places or grill places as well as restaurants.

The revenue was great in the year 2019 and ended that year with a good note. Our vision
statement at Lubanzi Piggery Farming is to be recognised as the top leading pig farming
business in the district and also to be counted amongst the top ten farming brands in the
Province. Our Mission statement in the industry is to become number one reputable brand in
the district that sells pigs and piglets, together with processed and packaged pork and other
pig products at an affordable price.
